## *What is this Chatbot?*
Artist.AI is a Persona-Based Conversational Agent. Unlike standard chatbots (like ChatGPT or Siri) which act as helpful assistants, Artist.AI is designed to roleplay. 
When a user selects an artist, the AI adopts the: 
1. The Vocabulary: Speaking in the dialect and tone of the late 19th century.
2. The Knowledge Base: Possessing deep knowledge of that specific artist's paintings, timeline, friends, and enemies.
3. The Psychology: This is a key feature shown in the UI. The bot doesn't just recite facts, it simulates the artist's emotional state, struggles with mental health, and artistic philosophy.
